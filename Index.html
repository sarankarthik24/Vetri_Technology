Key Features in UI/UX Designing

Wireframing

A layout of the product's interface elements on key pages. Wireframing is a crucial step in the interaction design process.

Information architecture

Users should be able to find information on a website or app without much effort.

Responsive design

Allows users to access content on any device. It considers factors like usability, accessibility, and scalability.

Interaction design

The connection between the users and the products. The goal is to create a product that meets user expectations and standards.

Prototyping

Designers can create a high or low fidelity prototype to test a feature, application, or website.

UX research skills

UX designers gather qualitative and quantitative data about users through research and analysis.

Consistency

Limits confusion, builds trust, and reinforces your brand.

Usability

The product should be easy to use, with a clear purpose and intuitive functionality.
